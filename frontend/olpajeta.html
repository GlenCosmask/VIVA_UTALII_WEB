Welcome to Ol Pejeta Conservancy

Discover Kenya's leading wildlife conservancy, home to the last two northern white rhinos and a sanctuary for endangered species.

Top Experiences in Ol Pejeta

Rhino Sanctuary Visit

Meet the last two northern white rhinos and learn about conservation efforts.

Game Drives

Spot the Big Five and other wildlife across the expansive conservancy.

Chimpanzee Sanctuary

Visit Sweetwaters Chimpanzee Sanctuary, home to rescued primates.

Lion Tracking

Join researchers to track and observe lions in their natural habitat.

Night Game Drives

Experience nocturnal wildlife with specialized spotlight tours.

Guided Bush Walks

Explore the conservancy on foot with experienced armed guides.

Conservation Center

Learn about cutting-edge wildlife conservation and research programs.

Bird Watching

Discover over 300 bird species in diverse habitats across the conservancy.
