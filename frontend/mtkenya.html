Top Experiences at Mt. Kenya

Mountain Climbing

Conquer Africa's second-highest peak and witness stunning views from above the clouds.

Scenic Hiking Trails

Traverse lush forests and alpine meadows filled with unique flora and fauna.

Wildlife Encounters

Spot elephants, buffalo, and rare mountain antelopes in their natural habitat.

Mountain Camping

Experience crisp alpine nights under a dazzling canopy of stars.

Nature Photography

Capture spectacular sunrise views and the mountain's iconic jagged peaks.

Bird Watching

Observe over 130 bird species, including rare endemic species.

Guided Tours

Local guides share stories, tracks, and safe routes across the park.

Stargazing

Low light pollution makes for exceptional night-sky viewing.
